## Rune-buyer traders (INVERSE economy)

These four **buy bulk items and pay out the non-Prison runes** — the opposite direction from every trader above (which sell goods for runes). They give players a non-dungeon faucet for the themed runes: dump surplus mats, get the matching rune. Each trade takes **two item groups** (buy + buyB slots) and outputs 1–4 runes. Input rarity scales with rune value (logs→Promenade, sculk/nether-star→Black Bridge). 12 trades each. Prison runes are NOT sold here — Prison stays the dungeon baseline. Same anchored/invulnerable NBT as all traders.
